The Easy Secret Pal Platform

This platform provides an easy way to manage a Secret Pal group and provide participants with online access to their Secret Pal's preferences and special day information.

Thank you for considering joining a Secret Pal Group

First things first - please enter the Group Name as provided from the Secret Pal Administrator in the invitation and enter your username into the fields below. (Please do so even if you don't want to participate, so you will not recieve further notifications or emails about upcoming fun activities.)

Please complete all questions on the survey below and agree to the terms/rules to participate as a Secret Pal. If you do not wish to participate, please click the No Thank You button below and the administrator will remove your information from the tables. If you change your mind - they can add you back if you do it before the secret pal matches are made.

Rules for Participating in Secret Pals

  • Secret Pal Gifts should be in the value range of $5.00 to $20.00 depending on the reason for celebration.
  • Secret Pal Gifts should be exchanged at least 3 times during the year.
  • Secret Pals will be revealed at the Christmas party scheduled in December with the final gift exchange at the Christmas party.
  • All gifts must be considered in good taste and comply with all the company's Harassment and Discrimination policies.
  • You must try to keep your identify a secret for the year until the reveal party. Please don't tell others who their secret pal is and don't ask who is your secret pal.
