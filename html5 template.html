Secret Pal Platform

Welcome to the Secrect Pal Platform

Congratulations on volunteering (or being volunteered) to the Secret Pal Administrator. This website is designed to make your life a little easier by making sending out notifications, matching users and managing the preference forms.

The Admin Cheat Sheet provides step by step instructions at a high level of things you should do.

The Secret Pal User is a page for end users to view their own preference sheet and view their matched Pal's Preference Sheet.

The rules help lay out participation and behaviour expectations.

The Preference Form is a survey completed for all agreed participants.
