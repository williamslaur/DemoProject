The Easy Secret Pal Platform

This platform provides an easy way to manage a Secret Pal group and provide participants with online access to their Secret Pal's preferences and special day information. If you want to create a new Secret Pal group, follow the instructions for Step 1 and then continue with the remaining steps. If you entered your admin email address and group on the front page, you should see see you have access to all tables and see the status of the participants.

Welcome to the Admin's Cheat Sheet

Congratulations on volunteering (or being volunteered) to the Secret Pal Administrator. This website is designed to make your life a little easier by making sending out notifications, matching users and managing the preference forms.

The Admin Cheat Sheet provides step by step instructions of things you should do. Open up each accordian below, follow the instructions, and you will be done in record time.

Secret Pal users will be returned to this site, but have their own pages which only allows them to either agree or decline becoming a secret pal, confirm they agree to the rules, and complete their own preference survey form. Once matched with another Secret Pal, then they will see their matches' survey answers.

Enter your email address and the emails of one to two other people who will assist with managing the group. The administrator can see all the information for secret pals, including their matches, and all preference surveys. If an administrator wants to participate, enter as a participant in another group.

In the Participants table only enter the email addresses of those you wish to participate. When they accept the terms and complete the survey, then you will be able to match the secret pals.

Create a set of rules to provide each secret pal. This should include a reference to the company's harrasment and discrimination policies. It should mention how often each secret pal should participate and if there are any limitations to dollar value of gifts.

    See the Examples:
  • Secret Pal Gifts should be in the value range of $5.00 to $20.00 depending on the reason for celebration.
  • Secret Pal Gifts should be exchanged at least 3 times during the year.
  • Secret Pals will be revealed at the Christmas party scheduled in December with the final gift exchange at the Christmas party.
  • All gifts must be considered in good taste and comply with all the company's Harassment and Discrimination policies.
  • You must try to keep your identify a secret for the year until the reveal party. Please don't tell others who their secret pal is and don't ask who is your secret pal.

This invitation will be sent to all the Secret Pal's who have not agreed to the rules, completed the survey and have not declined to participate. This is so you can send the email again as a reminder if needed. The email will include the Rules you created in Step 2.
