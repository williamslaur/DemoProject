The Easy Secret Pal Platform

This platform provides an easy way to manage a Secret Pal group and provide participants with online access to their Secret Pal's preferences and special day information. If you are a first time visitor, click the browse button to view the admin cheat sheet page that contains all the needed tools and instructions for easy set up. Otherwise, please sign in to continue.

Participant Sign In Box

Please enter your email address in the participant box and click the submit button to access your Secret Pal's preferences or your own.

Administrator Sign In Box

Please enter your email address in the administrator box and click submit to access all user information and manage the Secret Pal group.

Enter a valid email in only one box before clicking submit. An address must contain an @ and . to be valid.
