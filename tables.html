The Easy Secret Pal Platform

This platform provides an easy way to manage a Secret Pal group and provide participants with online access to their Secret Pal's preferences and special day information. If you want to create a new Secret Pal group, follow the instructions for Step 1 and then continue with the remaining steps. If you entered your admin email address and group on the front page, you should see see you have access to all tables and see the status of the participants.
